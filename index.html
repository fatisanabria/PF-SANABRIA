Hey!

Hey! My name is Karl (or Kaycem) and I'm a Filipino Freelance Artist living in Los Angeles. I've grown up watching countless cartoons and now I love to work on projects that inspire the next generation. I'd love to work with you on your next project! I've done works as a Character Designer, Concept Artist, and Illustrator for companies such as DeviantArt, Twitch, and AMD while also working with small businesses and content creators with their branding. Previously I worked as a Backend Software engineer but in 2020 I decided to go back to chasing my creative passions when I started my first art streams on Twitch. On top of working as a Freelance Character Designer, I teach art full time on my Twitch streams which you can catch Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays from 8 AM to 12 PM PST!
